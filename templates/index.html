Molar Mass Calculator

A small chemistry tool for calculating molar masses, reagent masses, and moles with precision formatting.

IUPAC Standards

Based on Atomic Weights of the Elements 1995 published by IUPAC. Contains atomic masses up to Uranium with scientific precision.

Advanced Parsing

Handles complex formulas with parentheses and nested structures. Examples: Ca(OH)₂, CH₃(CH₂)₃OH, Mg₃(PO₄)₂

Precision Results

Molar masses to 3 decimal places, reagent masses to 4 decimal places (analytical balance standard), moles to 6 decimal places.

Choose Calculation Mode

Mode 1: Calculate Molar Mass Only

Enter a chemical formula to calculate its molar mass in g/mol

Mode 2: Calculate Molar Mass and Reagent Mass

Calculate both molar mass and the mass needed for a given number of moles

Mode 3: Calculate Moles from Reagent Mass

Calculate the number of moles from a given mass of compound

About the MMCalc Project

Made by a proud Iskolar ng Bayan taking the BS Chemistry degree program in the University of the Philippines Los Baños to aid in the writing of laboratory reports for organic and analytical chemistry laboratory component courses.

Originally written in Python as a CLI program serving as a personal project and an application of learnings from CMSC 12: Foundations of Computer Science. Now rewritten as a Flask web app in Replit with AI-assisted web app development.
